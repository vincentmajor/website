Vincent Major is a PhD Candidate studying Medical Informatics at NYU Langone Health. Working with the Predictive Analytics Unit, a division of Population Health, he focuses on development, validation, and deployment of machine and deep learning models learnt from and feeding back into the electronic medical record.
